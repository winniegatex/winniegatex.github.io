Who I Am

Hey there! 👋 I'm Winfred Wambui — a backend developer who’s equally excited about efficient code and meaningful connections. From PHP and MySQL to dashboards and databases, I thrive in transforming complex data into smooth digital experiences.

But I'm not just about code. I'm a firm believer in human-centered tech. Every system I build is guided by a question: "How will this make life easier or better for someone?"

My journey started with curiosity and a passion for problem-solving. Along the way, I've worked with Fleek Developers and Vision Driven Org, fine-tuning everything from admin panels to technical support workflows. I'm also currently pursuing a BSc in IT at Kenyatta University.

My personal motto: Code with purpose. Connect with people. Keep exploring.
