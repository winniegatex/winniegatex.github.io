NAIROBI NATIONAL PARK – LAKE NAKURU NATIONAL PARK – AMBOSELI NATIONAL PARK

Nairobi

Duration

4 Days 3 Nights

Ages

All Ages+

Transport

Shuttle

Location

Nairobi

Overview

DAY 1

  • Pick up from Nairobi, Landcruiser Option
  • Departure for Nairobi National Park, a unique ecosystem by being the only protected area in the world close to a capital city. Located just 7kms from Nairobi’s city center, Nairobi National Park is the perfect place for a half-day or full day excursion or Tour from the Kenyan capital. One of the only places on earth where you can be on safari with skyscrapers as part of your backdrop, it’s an ideal layover escape or add-on to your existing safari.
  • Arrival and Check-in at the Park
  • Extensive half day Game Drive with a likelihood of seeing Rhinos, buffaloes, cheetah, zebras, giraffes, lions and plenty of antelopes and gazelles, Secretary bird, crowned cranes, vultures, peckers and many more roaming in these open plains with a section of highland forest as well as stretches of broken bush country, deep, rocky valleys and gorges with scrub and long grass.
  • After the game drive we depart for Nakuru
  • Arrival, Check-in with Dinner at the Hotel

DAY 2

  • Breakfast at the hotel
  • Half day Game Drive in L. Nakuru National Park. The game drive takes you to the lake shore to view Flamingoes and pelicans also spot White/Black Rhinos, Rothschild Giraffe, Water-bucks and other kinds of African wildlife. The Lake Forest attracts a wide variety of birds.
  • Served hot buffet Lunch at the hotel
  • Evening Game drive with an eye-catching Sunset view over the lake making it appear bloody red. Catch evening wildlife as they retire to their territories for the night while nocturnal one come out to hunt.
  • Back to the hotel, refresh and buffet Dinner is served

DAY 3

  • 10 am; Check out from hotel with breakfast
  • Morning game drive as we exit the Park.
  • Departure for Amboseli National Park where we will spend our day in a park romantically situated in the foothills of Mount Kilimanjaro. Relax and enjoy this idyllic African scene as you wind your way to Amboseli Park. Within a few hours we will arrive and start the adventure.
  • Check-in at the hotel with Lunch served
  • Afternoon Game drive at Amboseli National Park where we are likely to see the famous and tremendous variety of big game – an excellent place to view huge herds of Elephants, Lions, Cheetah, Wildebeest, Giraffes and Baboons. With Mt. Kilimanjaro as our background, you’ll see the herds of protected elephants, many sporting immense ivory tusks.
  • Back to the hotel, refresh, Dinner is served

DAY 4

  • Early Check out with Breakfast at the hotel
  • Have a Morning game drive as we exit the Park
  • Departure back to Nairobi. Having taken in the best of Amboseli, we head back to the highway and to the capital city of Nairobi, arriving in the evening.
